Pre-Incubation

The Pre-Incubation program is a customized program designed to provide first hand entrepreneurship development training for aspiring entrepreneurs with a business idea or a recent startup (less than one year) who need assistance developing a business plan.

Investment Award

competition takes place during the conference. The competition provides burgeoning entrepreneurs an opportunity to pitch their business to a panel of investors and industry experts for a chance to receive startup funding.

Entrepreneur Hub

The Entrepreneurship-Hub program is a customized program designed to provide first hand entrepreneurship development financial support for aspiring entrepreneurs with a business idea who need assistance developing a business plan.

Business Acceleration

CTA act as A business accelerator gives developing companies access to mentorship, investors and other support that help to become stable, self-sufficient businesses.

Skill To Enterprise

Enterprise skills are sought to build business in a way of creative, innovative mode and solving the problem, working independently regardless level of education to achieve the successful business.

TED Conference

TED Conference is a platform for Tibetan entrepreneurs to network, collaborate, and innovate. The annual conference provides Tibetan entrepreneurs an opportunity to gain insights and connect with business leaders, investors, and industry experts.
